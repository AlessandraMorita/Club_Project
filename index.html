For the Love of Plants

We are gardeners, friends, and family, we believe in sharing the beauty of plants. We love our greenhouses and playing in the dirt, but at the root of it all we are plant lovers.

Each month's box is assembled as if we were receiving it ourselves. We know what plant lovers dig because we dig plants!
